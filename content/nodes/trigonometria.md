# Trigonometria

La **trigonometria** studia le relazioni tra lati e angoli dei triangoli.

## Seno, coseno, tangente

$$ \sin(\theta) = \frac{\text{cateto opposto}}{\text{ipotenusa}} $$

$$ \cos(\theta) = \frac{\text{cateto adiacente}}{\text{ipotenusa}} $$

$$ \tan(\theta) = \frac{\sin(\theta)}{\cos(\theta)} = \frac{\text{cateto opposto}}{\text{cateto adiacente}} $$

## Valori notevoli

| Angolo | $\sin$ | $\cos$ | $\tan$ |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| $30^\circ$ | $1/2$ | $\sqrt{3}/2$ | $\sqrt{3}/3$ |
| $45^\circ$ | $\sqrt{2}/2$ | $\sqrt{2}/2$ | $1$ |
| $60^\circ$ | $\sqrt{3}/2$ | $1/2$ | $\sqrt{3}$ |
