# Geometria solida

## Cubo

$$ \text{Volume} = l^3 $$

$$ \text{Superficie} = 6l^2 $$

## Parallelepipedo

$$ \text{Volume} = a \times b \times h $$

$$ \text{Superficie} = 2(ab + ah + bh) $$

## Cilindro (con $\pi \approx 3$)

$$ \text{Volume} = \pi r^2 h \approx 3r^2 h $$

$$ \text{Superficie totale} = 2\pi r(r + h) \approx 6r(r + h) $$
